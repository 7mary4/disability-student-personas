Student Support Persona Cards

Practical strategies for supporting every learner in your classroom.

About This Resource

These 60 persona cards are designed for K–12 classroom teachers, special educators, and support staff. Each card centers a specific disability, chronic health condition, or learning difference, offering grade-band observations, strengths, accommodations, assistive technology recommendations, and IEP considerations. This resource is grounded in the principles of Universal Design for Learning (UDL) and the protections established under IDEA (Individuals with Disabilities Education Act) to help educators understand, anticipate, and meaningfully support students with disabilities across all settings.
